Sound Radio Ready

Tier 1 publishing potential lives and dies on effective and powerful songwriting. Teams build dreams- that's why Justin Beiber, Selena Gomez, Usher, and nearly every other A-List player creates music with others.
The idea that "combined effort generates incredible art" is the lynchpin to how we operate at Brownout Studios.
Writing with us allows you to concentrate on the best parts of what you do. If that's lyric writing, melody writing, production/parts, etc: we've got you covered.

Co-Write

We are co-writers together on the track! This is a great option because not only do we help you write the best song we possibly can together, but we also help you take the song to the next level. Here's how it works...
Total cost is $650 USD (includes all taxes). With a $300 down payment we get to work on your track that matches your original concepts. If you do not like our first pass, we will write another track or revise the first based on your suggestions. If by chance you still do not like the ideas we have provided to you, we part as friends.
Assuming you will be like all our other customers, you will love the production and quick demo we provide on the first pass. From there, you pay the remainder of the balance due and we get to work on creating a finished radio-ready product.
If you're not the singer / performer of the track, then you can either hire your own singer, or we can also provide a great demo singer at an additional $160 USD. (In some cases we can leverage our relationships with other singer/songwriters and find a performer of the song which helps to increase the songs earning potential.)
Once all performances for the track are done, we will help mix and master the song with no additional costs. We will then non-exclusively add the completed work to our publishing library and help you get the song into paying opportunities. All of the details will be put into a Split Sheet Agreement with the agreed upon splits.
