Song Representation

Brownout Studios is also a licensing agent. An agent represents your music solely on an opportunity basis. We actively search for and build relationships with music supervisors, publishers, other agents, and ad agencies, (to name a few) in order to create paid placements for your music. Here's how it works...

You can either submit single tracks below, or you can provide us syncable access to your entire library. We then listen to each and every song submission to make sure it is the quality we know our constituents and clients will want.

After careful consideration, we will provide you with a licensing agreement for one year to NON-EXCLUSIVELY manage the individual track or collection of tracks selected from your submission. You can keep submitting more tracks as you get them ready.

Songs in our catalog are appropriately meta tagged and easy to search within our database. We meticulously cycle through each of the songs in our catalog on a regular basis to make sure proper tagging has been completed. This will make sure your tracks have the best possible chance of being "discovered" by our clients and staff sync agents.

We will then inform you of any and all opportunities as they come up and payouts will happen as per the signed agreements. As a Sync Agent, we only collect the Sync Fees up front. There is no retitling of your music, no publishing constraints, and no backend royalties due to us after the initial deal has been made. It does not get any simpler than that
